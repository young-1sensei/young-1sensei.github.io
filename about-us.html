About our center

Medicaid Radio-Diagnostics & Clinics has been serving Abuja and its environs since 2009. We are patient focused, community oriented, diagnostics center fully equipped with technology that rivals the nation’s most prestigious healthcare systems. We prioritize, your comfort and wellbeing is our priority. All of our physicians are board certified and skilled in various subspecialties. Our technologists are certified and registered in their areas of specialty and all of our facilities are fully accredited by relevant health agencies.
