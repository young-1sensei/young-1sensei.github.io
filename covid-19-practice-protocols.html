Fight against covid-19

To Our Patients:

As we navigate through these historic and uncertain times, we are committed to our patients and providing high-quality care. Our office is following all recommended guidelines from public health authorities and the CDC including best practices for Infection Control, hygiene and medical professional team health in the fight against COVID-19.

Medicaid is confident in our ability to see patients and provide you with high-quality, efficient, individualized, and safe care in the future.

CORONAVIRUS NOTICE TO PATIENTS:

If you have been diagnosed with coronavirus or are experiencing any of the following symptoms:

  1. Fever of 99 degrees or higher
  2. A new cough and/or a new onset of shortness of breath
  3. Recent loss of sense of smell and/or taste
  4. New gastrointestinal symptoms, especially diarrhea
  5. Sore throat
  6. DVT or abnormal clotting

Or - If you have been directly exposed to someone with confirmed or suspected coronavirus and have been told to self-quarantine, do not come to our office. We cannot perform your examination until 14 days has passed from that initial exposure. Please call us at 07030044300 to notify us and to reschedule your appointment.
